Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, roll number, and academic details when you register for an account. We also collect information about your book requests and library activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide library services, process book requests, send notifications about your requests, and improve our services. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely using AWS cloud services with encryption and access controls.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the library administration at Greenfield University.
